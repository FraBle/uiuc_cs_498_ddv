Interactive Slideshow for CS 498 DDV

By Frank Blechschmidt

This interactive slideshow explores the results of the Stack Overflow Developer Survey from 2019.

It lets the viewer explore different facets to answer the question ”How to Succeed in a Career in Tech?”. It highlights which programming languages have the highest popularity, how this translates to compensation based on work experience, and finally explores the compensation in a global context.

  • Data Set
  • Filtering
Stack Overflow’s annual Developer Survey is the largest and most comprehensive survey of people who code around the world. Each year, we field a survey covering everything from developers’ favorite technologies to their job preferences. This year marks the ninth year we’ve published our annual Developer Survey results, and nearly 90,000 developers took the 20-minute survey earlier this year.
Source: Stack Overflow

The data of every chart can be filtered by age, compensation in USD and gender. These filters are implemented to work across all charts: Their values affect the visualized data on all 3 slides of this interactive slideshow simultaneously.

Note: The filtering and crunching of the numbers is happening in the browser and can be quite compute-intense since the data set contains almost 90,000 responses.
